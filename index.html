Kiana Sardari

Media and Cultural Studies

Born and raised in Los Angeles, media has always been an important part of my life. I have participated in the arts for over 15 years, including an education in theatre and acting, voice, and dance. I trained at Los Angeles Ballet Academy for twelve years, where I passed annual examinations from the Royal Academy of Dance.

In recent years, I have developed a passion for working with social media. Since July 2019, I have been managing an Instagram account (@marianelanunez_ballet) for Royal Ballet principal dancer, Marianela Nunez. In January of 2021, I launched an Instagram blog (@studioamag) focused on promoting mental health in the dance community. Additionally, I have worked as a social media assistant for actors to help them gain a following and promote their projects. Aside from media, I work with children as a tutor and assistant ballet teacher. I have learned organizational skills as well as how to be a leader and manage my time.

In the future, I hope to work in entertainment and media to highlight important stories.
